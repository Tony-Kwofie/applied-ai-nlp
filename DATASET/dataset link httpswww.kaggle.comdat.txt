OSHA Severe Injury Reports (2015–2022)

Source: Kaggle – OSHA Severe Injury Reports

Format: CSV

Description: Contains records of severe workplace injuries, including narrative descriptions of incidents, injury types (e.g., amputations, hospitalizations), employer information, and industry codes.

Relevance: Provides unstructured text suitable for NLP tasks like classifying incidents by type or industry, making it ideal for building and evaluating machine learning models in occupational health and safety.
